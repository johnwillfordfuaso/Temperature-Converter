The formula F = 1.8C + 32 is used to convert Fahrenheit to Celsius.
The formula C = (F - 32) / 1.8 is used to convert Celsius to Fahrenheit.
The formula C = K - 273.15 is used to convert Kelvin to Celsius.
The formula K = C + 273.15 is used to convert Celsius to Kelvin.
